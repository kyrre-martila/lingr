# Lingr — Concept Summary

Lingr is a slow dating app built around one belief:

**Some people are worth taking time with.**

Most dating apps are designed around speed:
- fast judgments
- endless swiping
- constant comparison
- high engagement
- infinite choice

People become profiles. Profiles become content.

Lingr is designed to do the opposite.

Instead of showing hundreds of people, Lingr shows only a small number of carefully selected people each day. The goal is not to maximize interaction. The goal is to create the conditions for genuine curiosity, emotional presence, and gradual connection.

Lingr is not built around instant attraction alone.
It is built around the idea that interest can deepen over time when people are given enough space to become known.

---

# Core Philosophy

Lingr is designed around:

- intentional connection
- emotional presence
- slower pacing
- fewer people
- gradual trust
- depth over volume

The app should feel:
- calm
- warm
- reflective
- emotionally intelligent
- human

It should never feel:
- addictive
- loud
- gamified
- performative
- overwhelming
- marketplace-driven

Lingr does not optimize for maximum screen time.
If two people meet, build something meaningful, and leave the app, that is considered success.

# Core Concepts

## Glimps

A **Glimps** is a small moment from someone’s world.

A Glimps is not just a photo.
It is a unified moment card:
- image or short video
- reflection text
- optional audio or music

The focus is not self-presentation.
The focus is atmosphere, emotion, reflection, and presence.

Examples:
- rain against a window
- a quiet morning coffee
- a favorite place
- a book
- a thought
- a piece of music
- a moment that felt meaningful

Glimps are designed to communicate:
“How does this person experience the world?”

not:
“How well can this person market themselves?”

Selfies are allowed later through Layers, but discovery is built around moments first.

---

## Spark

A **Spark** is an intentional expression of interest.

It is not a swipe.
It is not a “like.”
It is not instant matching.

A user sends a Spark without knowing whether the other person will return it.

When someone receives a Spark, they are told:
> “Someone dwelled on you.”

They can see the sender’s Glimps and emotional profile, but not their identity.

Only when both users Spark each other does a Connection open.

Sparks are intentionally limited each day.
They should feel meaningful and deliberate, not disposable.

---

## Connection

A **Connection** is created when two Sparks are mutual.

This unlocks:
- chat
- deeper profile access
- Layer progression
- shared experiences inside the app

Connections are meant to feel slower and more focused than typical matches.

The goal is not:
“collect matches.”

The goal is:
“explore whether something real exists between two people.”

---

## Layers

**Layers** are one of the core systems in Lingr.

Instead of revealing an entire profile immediately, information unlocks gradually as two people invest in the Connection.

At first, users mainly see:
- Glimps
- reflections
- emotional energy
- values
- atmosphere

As the Connection deepens, more becomes visible:
- names
- clearer photos
- interests
- stories
- personal values
- Pulse history
- deeper profile details

Layer progression is based on:
- meaningful conversation
- mutual activity
- Pulse participation
- continued presence over time

Layers are never unlocked through payment.

**Layers reward investment, not time.**

---

## Pulse

**Pulse** is a daily reflection system.

Each day, users receive a small reflective question.

Examples:
- “What made you feel calm today?”
- “What made you smile?”
- “What have you been thinking about lately?”
- “What felt meaningful today?”

Pulse is not:
- a quiz
- compatibility scoring
- streak mechanics
- personality testing

It is a quiet accumulation of presence.

Over time, Pulse answers help users feel that they know someone gradually and naturally.

Pulse history becomes shared at deeper Layers.

---

## Window

A **Window** is a deeper shared space inside a Connection.

Window becomes available when:
- a Connection has reached deeper Layers
- both users intentionally opt in

Window represents:
- focus
- mutual attention
- emotional intention

Inside a Window:
- discovery can pause or slow down
- more profile depth becomes visible
- shared prompts appear
- users can collect moments together
- date ideas and deeper reflection become more central

Window is not exclusivity enforcement.
It is a mutual decision to give a Connection more space and attention.

---

## Snuggle

**Snuggle** is a real-time co-presence feature available inside Window.

Both users hold a button at the same time for as long as they want.

There are:
- no points
- no streaks
- no rewards
- no gamification

It is simply a small moment of synchronized presence.

Snuggle is intentionally ephemeral.
Nothing is stored permanently.

The feature exists to create:
- closeness
- stillness
- awareness of another person being present with you right now

# Presence

Presence is the emotional foundation of Lingr.

The app is designed to reward:
- attention
- curiosity
- emotional investment
- consistency
- reflection

—not popularity, performance, or speed.

The algorithm is not meant to judge people.
It is meant to guide people toward compatible:
- pacing
- values
- energy
- intentions
- geography

---

# Monetization Philosophy

Lingr’s monetization model is intentionally limited.

Core emotional depth is never paywalled.

Features like:
- Layers
- Window
- Snuggle
- deeper connection progression

are available to all users.

Paid subscriptions only increase breadth of discovery.

Example:
- Free users: 1 curated person per day
- Paid users: 3–5 curated people per day

The philosophy is:

> Depth is always free.

Users should never feel pressured to pay in order to emotionally connect with another person.

---

# Privacy Philosophy

Lingr is privacy-conscious by design.

The app intentionally limits identity exposure during discovery.

Before mutual Connection:
- no names
- no exact age
- no raw location
- no user IDs
- no full profile visibility

Profiles open gradually through Layers.

Location data is used only for matching and is never exposed directly.

Media is served through signed temporary URLs.

Lingr should feel emotionally safe, not socially exposing.

---

# The Goal

Lingr is not trying to maximize engagement.

It is trying to create the conditions for meaningful human connection.

The app exists to help people:
- notice each other
- become curious about each other
- gradually become known to each other
- and potentially build something real outside the app

Lingr is not an app for endlessly browsing people.

Lingr is an app for dwelling a little longer on a few.
