# Run 9 — Discovery UI MVP

## UX decisions
- Single-introduction presentation to support intentional focus.
- Editorial hierarchy: name, place, reflection, interests/energies, layer hint.
- Primary action is gentle Spark send; secondary is quiet pass.

## Calm interaction rationale
- No swiping and no stacked cards.
- Soft visual language with minimal motion.
- Confirmation feedback is understated and non-gamified.

## Deferred recommendation engine
- Current rollout relies on backend discovery candidate contract and excludes an advanced recommendation layer.
- Future work: ranking quality, energy matching refinements, and adaptive pacing tuning.

## Lingr vs Tinder constraints
- No rejection-energy copy.
- No timer pressure or urgency patterns.
- No dopamine-loop animations.
- Intentional pacing over rapid browsing metaphors.
